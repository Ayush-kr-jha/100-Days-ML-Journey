# Applications of Machine Learning

Machine Learning is used to make systems **smarter, faster, and more predictive**
across many real-world domains.



## Retail & E-Commerce
- Product recommendations (Amazon, Flipkart)
- Demand forecasting and inventory planning
- Dynamic pricing



## Healthcare
- Disease detection using medical data
- Medical image analysis
- Personalized treatment recommendations



## Banking & Finance
- Fraud detection
- Credit scoring and risk assessment
- Algorithmic trading



## Transportation & Logistics
- Route optimization
- Traffic prediction
- Self-driving and assisted vehicles



## Manufacturing
- Predictive maintenance
- Defect detection
- Process optimization



## Marketing & Advertising
- Customer segmentation
- Targeted ads
- Sentiment analysis on reviews & social media



## Key Takeaway
> ML turns data into **decisions, predictions, and automation**.
