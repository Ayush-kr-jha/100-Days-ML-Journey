# Applications of ML 

1. Retail and E-commerce:
   - Personalized recommendations based on user behavior and preferences.
   - Inventory management and demand forecasting.
2. Healthcare:
    - Disease diagnosis and prognosis using medical imaging and patient data.
    - Drug discovery and personalized treatment plans.
3. Banking and Finance:
    - Fraud detection and risk assessment.
    - Algorithmic trading and portfolio management.
4. Transportation and Logistics:
    - Route optimization and predictive maintenance.
    - Autonomous vehicles and traffic management.
5. Manufacturing (Predictive Maintenance):
    - Predicting equipment failures to reduce downtime.
    - Quality control and process optimization.
6. Marketing and Advertising:
    - Customer segmentation and targeted advertising.
    - Sentiment analysis and brand monitoring.
